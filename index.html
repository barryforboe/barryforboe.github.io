My Priorities


  • Strive to reach out and engage community more about the BR educational system.
  • Promote innovation and creative methods for BR staff.
  • Inspire and praise the exceptional educators.
  • Pursue a plan to bolster kindergarten to full day and add enrich the early educational program.
  • Work on methods to manage budget to meet common core curriculum and community needs.
  • Build on the great foundation of the district to improve and excel for educational excellence.
