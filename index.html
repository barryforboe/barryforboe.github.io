Why I want to run

I believe that people should be encouraged to ask questions, never accept things as status quo. Beyond just asking questions, finding answers and potential solutions is equally important. That philosophy prompted me to run and to successfully be elected to the Bridgewater Raritan Regional School District (BRRSD) Board of Education (BOE) in 2014. Serving on the BOE has been quite a privilege. I do my best to perform this duty at the highest level. I have been an effective and collaborative board member. Serving on numerous BOE committees and being the board liaison to numerous foundations and commissions. This provided me the opportunity to witness and contribute to different aspects and organizations which are supporting and interacting with the district. Though my work on the board I have tried to do what is best for the students and community. My commitment, experience, focus and collaborate approach makes me the best choice for the Board of Education. Please vote for Walker.
